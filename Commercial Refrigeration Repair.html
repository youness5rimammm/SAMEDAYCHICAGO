Here are some common Commercial Refrigerator problems!

Refrigerator may have stopped working When your commercial refrigerator stops working, there may be some problems in the thermostat. SameDay Refrigeration Repair can help you by replacing the thermostat that is not working.

Frost in Freezers:Due to any kind of fault in the defrost system, there can be a lot of frost in your freezer which creates problems. Our technicians will fully replace the evaporator fan motor to fix the issue in your commercial refrigerator.

Water LeakageIf there is any kind of water leakage in your refrigerator due to a clogged drain hose or defrost drain opening, Same day Chicago refrigeration will help you out by replacing the old parts with new ones.

Refrigerator makes strange noises: If the compressor motor or fan is not working properly, then there are chances that your refrigerator makes strange noises. Our professional experts will fix the motor so that your refrigerator works efficiently and does not make any noise.

Food compartments have fluid: Sometimes due to old or damaged door seals, fluid gets accumulated in the fresh food compartments of the refrigerator. Our experts will help you by providing full replacement of the old seals.

Having Trouble In Cooling:If your refrigerator is having trouble in cooling and temperature maintenance, then our technicians will repair and fix any kind of issue with its thermostat, door gasket or a blocked drain that is not working properly.
